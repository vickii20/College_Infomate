About the College

Excellence in Engineering Education

Kongunadu College of Engineering and Technology (KNCET) is a premier educational institution established in 2007, offering quality education in engineering and technology. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty, we are committed to producing industry-ready engineers.
